Internet als Religion

Increase in Internet Usage in Germany

  • 58 million Germans were online in 2016.
  • 84% of Germans aged 14 and older use the internet.
  • Internet users increased by 2 million.
  • Most use their smartphones to go online.
  • 65% use the internet daily.

Mobile Internet Use and Age Differences

  • 28% use the internet on mobile devices daily.
  • 64% of people under 30 use mobile internet.
  • Traditional TV is less popular now.
  • Young people prefer watching videos on mobile devices.

Internet Use and Time Spent Online

  • Germans spend an average of 2 hours and 8 minutes online daily.
  • This has increased by 20 minutes from the previous year.
  • Mobile users spend 2 hours and 43 minutes online.
  • 39% of online time is for communication, 25% for media.

Changes in Media Consumption

  • Traditional TV is still most common but declining among the young.
  • Young people favor online videos.
  • ZDF updated its media library to attract younger viewers.
  • 14- to 29-year-olds are leading the rise in online video use.
